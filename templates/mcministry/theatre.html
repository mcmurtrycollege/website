Theatre

The theater committee is dedicated to both hosting quality theater shows at McMurtry, including productions with Kinda Sketchy and the Rice Players as well as its own "Theater in the Round". We hope to create new theater traditions at McMurtry, such as hosting workshops on auditioning and directing musicals and plays. Most of all we want to facilitate the creativity of students at McMurtry and help each and every one on their journey of discovery in the theatrical world.
