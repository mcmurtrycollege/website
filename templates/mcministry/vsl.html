Varsity Sports Liaison

Varsity Sports Liaisons are responsible for promoting Rice athletics at McMurtry. VSLs work with the Athletic Department to communicate Rice athletic events and promotions to the college, as well as organize tailgates for Murts to enjoy games together. It is our goal to make sure that Murts are always informed and excited about upcoming varsity athletic events, so if you have any ideas about ways we can do so, let us know!
