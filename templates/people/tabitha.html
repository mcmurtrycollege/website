Office Information

Office Hours

There are times the office will be closed. These may include, emergency, illness, meetings, university holidays, or personal time off. I will attempt to use student workers to cover SOME of those. However, as is the case with most other colleges, the office may not be open all day during these time periods. When the office is closed a notice will be placed on the door, and when possible a notice sent to the listserv. Student workers work in the afternoon and close the office.

Packages

There are not set delivery times for packages. I am not aware and do not control when deliveries will arrive. If you are expecting a package that you need that day, let me know ahead of time. If I am aware of an urgent need and will not be here, alternate arrangements may be may made.

The office is not responsible for any packages that are delivered and placed in the package cabinet. If you are missing a package, I am happy to assist you in locating the package.

In the event of my absence, I will attempt to make arrangements for packages to be delivered to another college. Otherwise, they will be sent to Delivery Services and delivered to McMurtry the following day. You will be notified of any alternate pickup options via a notice on the door and/or email.

Key Service

Key service is available through the office. If no one is in the office and you need key service, contact Housing at 713-348-5440. RUPD handles key service in the evening. You may contact RUPD at 713-348-6000.
