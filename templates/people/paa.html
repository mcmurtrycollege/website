Peer Academic Advisors

Peer Academic Advisors (PAAs) are fellow Murts who have been trained by the Office of Academic Advising to give academic advice on a wide range of topics. These are the students to talk to if you have questions about class registration, graduation requirements, declaring a major, research, study abroad, and so much more!
