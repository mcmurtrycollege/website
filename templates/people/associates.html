McMurtry Associates

McMurtry associates are Rice faculty as well as adult professionals in the Houston area who are associated with Rice. They serve as mentors, friends, academic advisors, and general resources for students. Associates participate in McMurtry life by hosting O-Week group dinners and outings, providing advice on careers or college life, and serving as guides to life after Rice.

Faculty Associates

These associates are Rice professors passionate about helping students have the best experience at college, both inside and outside the classroom. Use them as a source for academic or general college advice. Faculty associates also serve as faculty mentors and divisional advisors during O-Week and are the first point of contact for new students looking for advice from Rice professors.
