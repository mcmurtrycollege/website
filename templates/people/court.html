McMurtry College Court

The McMurtry Court is tasked with the responsibility of helping to keep McMurtry a safe and fair place to live for all of its Murts. We are responsible for dealing with code of conduct issues and other complaints within McMurtry. We serve as a liason with SJP and RUPD to help correct and eliminate issues before needing RUPD or SJP (Student Judicial Programs) to intervene. The Court has experience with a large number of different issues, ranging from noise complaints to seeking medical help if a student is in danger, so we can assist with finding the help the student needs. The justices are always on call and serve as a great resource for all of the members of McMurtry.
